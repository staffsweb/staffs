                    <div class="grid  grid--spaced">
                        <div class="col-3/4@tablet-wide">
                            <h3 class="h4  margin-top-none">Undergraduate (bachelor's) degrees</h3>

                            <p>To apply for an undergraduate (bachelors) degree you need one of the following qualifications:</p>

                            <ul>
                                <li>Obtain 2.3 in the Reifeprufungszeugnis/ Maturazeugnis</li>
                                <li>International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma with 28 points overall</li>
                                <li>Successful completion of a relevant International Foundation Year from a recognised UK Institution</li>
                                <li>For A-Level requirements, please see the entry requirements section on the relevant course page</li>
                            </ul>

                            <h3 class="h4">Integrated master's and two-year accelerated degrees</h3>

                            <p>To apply for an integrated masters or two-year accelerated degree you need one of the following qualifications:</p>

                            <ul>
                                <li>Obtain 2.0. in the Reifeprufungszeugnis/ Maturazeugnis</li>
                                <li>International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma with 30 points overall</li>
                                <li>Successful completion of a relevant International Foundation Year from a recognised UK Institution</li>
                                <li>For A-Level requirements, please see the entry requirements section on the relevant course page</li>
                            </ul>

                            <p>Some of our courses may require more specific qualifications. For the exact entry requirements for your course, please see the individual course pages. If you are not sure and would like further help, please contact us.</p>

                            <h3 class="h4">English language requirements</h3>

                            <ul>
                                <li>2.0. in the English module of the Reifeprufungszeugnis/ Maturazeugnis</li>
                            </ul>
